Project - Brisbane Pet Rescue

Note: Website is no longer active and is currently private (another team member has access to privacy controls on GitHub)

This is an innovative dog adoption website. Unlike competitors, the site includes a personality quiz to point users to their most compatible breeds and extensive filters covering a variety of desirable attributes. The site also has state-of-the-art post adoption features like medication reminders, location and user ratings of nearby dog parks and vets directly from a Google Maps API.

This website was completed in a group of 6, where I acted as one of the lead front-end developers and was heavily involved in UI/UX design and getting user evaluations.

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, MySQL, Photoshop, Figma, Git, GitHub, Google Maps API
